About the agency

Blossom Studios represents a focused board of talent for clients who value character, culture, and craft as much as image.

Built on a studio mindset, Blossom Studios acts as a steady partner to both models and clients — guiding careers with intention and casting projects with care.

We work closely with designers, photographers, and producers to place the right faces in the right stories: runway collections, editorial features, lookbooks, campaigns, and digital-first content.

Our work centers Chicano culture, heritage, and community, making room for talent to be fully themselves on and off set.
